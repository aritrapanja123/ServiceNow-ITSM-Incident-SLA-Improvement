# AS-IS Incident Management Process

## Purpose

This document describes the current AS-IS incident management process for NorthBridge Retail Group, a fictional UK retail organisation using ServiceNow to manage IT incidents.

The purpose of the AS-IS process map is to identify where delays, manual handoffs, incorrect assignments, SLA breaches and recurring incidents occur.

---

## AS-IS Process Overview

```text
User identifies IT issue
        ↓
User raises incident in ServiceNow
        ↓
Incident category and subcategory selected manually
        ↓
Impact and urgency selected manually
        ↓
Priority is assigned
        ↓
Incident is routed to assignment group
        ↓
Support analyst reviews ticket
        ↓
First response sent to user
        ↓
Analyst investigates issue
        ↓
Ticket may be reassigned if routed incorrectly
        ↓
Resolution or workaround provided
        ↓
Incident is marked as resolved
        ↓
User confirms resolution or ticket auto-closes
        ↓
SLA result is recorded
        ↓
Management reviews SLA performance manually
